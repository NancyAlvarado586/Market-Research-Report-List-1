<p><p>The Industrial Dental 3D Printer market is primarily categorized into Stereolithography (SLA), Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Digital Light Processing (DLP), and other technologies. SLA utilizes a laser to cure liquid resin into solid forms, producing high-detail models. FDM extrudes thermoplastic filaments layer by layer, ideal for sturdy prototypes. DLP employs a digital light projector to cure resin quickly, offering speed and precision. Other methods may include material jetting and binder jetting, catering to various dental applications and material requirements.</p></p>
