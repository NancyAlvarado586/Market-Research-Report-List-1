<p><p>The PVC stabilizer market comprises several types, each serving unique performance needs. Lead stabilizers, known for their cost-effectiveness and stability under heat, are facing regulatory challenges due to health concerns. Mixed metal stabilizers combine different metal salts to enhance thermal stability while minimizing environmental impact. Tin stabilizers offer non-toxic alternatives with excellent stability at high temperatures but come at a higher cost. Organic stabilizers, derived from renewable resources, provide eco-friendly options and are gaining popularity as sustainability becomes a priority.</p></p>
