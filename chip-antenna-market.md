<p><ul><li>Dielectric Chip Antennas</li><li>LTCC Chip Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chip antenna market primarily includes two types: dielectric chip antennas and LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) chip antennas. Dielectric chip antennas utilize a dielectric material to enhance performance while maintaining a compact size, making them suitable for various wireless applications. LTCC chip antennas, on the other hand, integrate multiple layers of ceramic materials, enabling miniaturization and improved electrical properties. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient and space-saving antennas in devices like smartphones, IoT devices, and wearables.</p></p>
