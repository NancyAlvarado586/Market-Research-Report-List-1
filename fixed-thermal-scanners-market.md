<p><p>The fixed thermal scanners market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners excel in detecting moisture and identifying features in a variety of materials. MWIR scanners are ideal for high-temperature detection and are often used in military applications. LWIR scanners, which operate at lower temperatures, are prevalent in surveillance, automation, and building diagnostics, as they effectively detect heat variations in a wide range of environments.</p></p>
