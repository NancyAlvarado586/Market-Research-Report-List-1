<p><p>The semiconductor bonding machine market is primarily segmented into two types: wire bonders and die bonders. Wire bonders are used to connect semiconductor devices through fine wires, providing electrical interconnections for integrated circuits. In contrast, die bonders focus on attaching semiconductor die to substrates or packages, ensuring thermal and electrical conductivity. Both types play crucial roles in the semiconductor manufacturing process, enabling the production of reliable and efficient electronic components essential for various applications across industries.</p></p>
