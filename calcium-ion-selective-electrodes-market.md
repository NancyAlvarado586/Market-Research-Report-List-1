<p><p>Calcium ion selective electrodes (ISEs) are critical for measuring calcium ion concentrations in various applications. The market features two primary types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a hydrophobic organic solvent containing ionophores, allowing for selective calcium ion transport. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes incorporate a plasticized polyvinyl chloride matrix, which also facilitates calcium ion selectivity but offers enhanced stability and durability. Both types are essential for environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and food quality assessment.</p></p>
