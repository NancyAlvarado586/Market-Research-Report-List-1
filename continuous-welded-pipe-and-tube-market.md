<p><p>The Continuous Welded Pipe and Tube market is characterized by two primary types: Straight Seam Welded Pipe and Helical Welded Pipe. Straight Seam Welded Pipe is produced by welding flat steel plates into cylindrical forms, typically used for applications requiring high pressure and structural integrity. In contrast, Helical Welded Pipe features a spiral seam, allowing for greater flexibility and reduced material usage. Both types cater to various industries, including oil and gas, construction, and water distribution, emphasizing durability and efficiency.</p></p>
