<p><p>The automotive smart key fob market primarily includes Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without removing the key fob from their pockets, enhancing convenience and security. In contrast, RKES requires the user to press a button on the fob to unlock or start the vehicle. Both systems improve vehicle accessibility and security, catering to the evolving preferences of consumers for advanced automotive technology.</p></p>
