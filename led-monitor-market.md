<p><strong>The LED Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Lit LED Monitor</li><li>Edge Lit LED monitor</li><li>Full Array LED Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED monitor market features three main types: Direct Lit LED monitors, which use LEDs behind the entire screen for uniform brightness; Edge Lit LED monitors, where LEDs are positioned along the edges, allowing for thinner designs but potentially less consistent lighting; and Full Array LED monitors, which have a grid of LEDs across the back, offering superior contrast and enhanced local dimming capabilities. Each type caters to different consumer needs regarding brightness, contrast, and design preferences.</p></p>
