<p><p>The helical CT scanner market is categorized into two main types: double-layer helical CT scanners and multi-layer helical CT scanners. Double-layer helical CT scanners utilize two detector arrays to capture images, improving speed and image quality for diagnostic purposes. In contrast, multi-layer helical CT scanners feature multiple detector layers, allowing for faster imaging and higher resolution by capturing several slices simultaneously. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities in medical imaging, catering to different clinical requirements and applications in healthcare.</p></p>
