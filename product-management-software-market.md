<p><p>Product management software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and security, but necessitating significant maintenance and upfront costs. Conversely, cloud-based software operates on a subscription model, hosted on the vendor's servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from any location. This model allows for seamless updates and collaboration but relies on internet connectivity and raises potential concerns over data privacy.</p></p>
