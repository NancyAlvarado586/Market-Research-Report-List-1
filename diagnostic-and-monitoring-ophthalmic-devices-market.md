Fundus cameras capture images of the retina, tonometers measure intraocular pressure, and ophthalmoscopes allow for direct observation of the eye's interior. Slit lamps offer a magnified view of the eye’s front structures, and other devices encompass various ancillary tools for comprehensive eye care diagnostics.</p></p>
