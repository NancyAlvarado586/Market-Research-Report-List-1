<p><p>The ultrasound market is primarily divided into two types: diagnostic ultrasound and therapeutic ultrasound. Diagnostic ultrasound uses high-frequency sound waves to create images of internal body structures, aiding in the detection and monitoring of various medical conditions, including pregnancy and organ health. In contrast, therapeutic ultrasound utilizes sound waves for treatment purposes, such as breaking down kidney stones, tissue repair, and pain relief. Both sectors are essential in modern medicine, enhancing patient care through non-invasive techniques.</p></p>
