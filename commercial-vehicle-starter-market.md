<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Starter Market is primarily categorized into two types: Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. Direct Control starters operate using a simple mechanical system that engages the starter motor directly when the ignition is activated, ensuring immediate and reliable performance. Electromagnetic Control starters, on the other hand, utilize electromagnetic mechanisms to engage the starter, providing enhanced efficiency and smoother operation. Both types are essential for initiating engine function in commercial vehicles, each offering unique advantages suitable for various applications in the industry.</p></p>
