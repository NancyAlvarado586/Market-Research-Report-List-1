<p><p>The ultrasonic distance sensor market can be categorized into two main types: analog and digital sensors. Analog type sensors provide continuous voltage output proportional to the distance measured, enabling gradual distance variations to be captured. In contrast, digital type sensors offer discrete readings, typically outputting distance in predefined units, which simplifies integration with digital systems. Both types are widely used in applications such as robotics, automotive, and industrial automation, each serving different requirements based on precision and data processing needs.</p></p>
