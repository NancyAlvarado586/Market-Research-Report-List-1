<p><p>The laser communication equipment market encompasses various components essential for transmitting and receiving information via laser beams. Transmitters convert electrical signals into optical signals, facilitating data transfer. Receivers perform the inverse function, capturing optical signals and converting them back to electrical form. Modulators manipulate the intensity, phase, or frequency of the laser light to encode information, while demodulators decode the received signals for interpretation. Together, these elements enable efficient, high-speed communication systems in various applications, including telecommunications and satellite communications.</p></p>
