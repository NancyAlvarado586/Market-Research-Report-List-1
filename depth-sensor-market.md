<p><strong>The Depth Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Sensing</li><li>Passive Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The depth sensor market is categorized into active sensing and passive sensing technologies. Active sensing employs emitted signals, such as infrared light or laser, to measure depth by analyzing reflections, offering high accuracy and performance in various lighting conditions. In contrast, passive sensing relies on ambient light and existing scene illumination, capturing depth through image processing techniques without active emission. Both technologies cater to different applications, from augmented reality to robotics, each with distinct advantages based on environmental conditions and required precision.</p></p>
