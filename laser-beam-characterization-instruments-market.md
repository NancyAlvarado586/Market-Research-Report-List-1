Beam quality measurement devices assess parameters like M² factor, providing insights into the laser's focusability and efficiency. Wavefront measurement devices evaluate the phase front of the laser beam, crucial for high-precision applications. Additionally, other instruments may include diagnostics and monitoring tools, catering to diverse industry needs in laser technology.</p></p>
