<p><strong>The Handheld Medical Infrared Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forehead Type Thermometer</li><li>Ear Type Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld medical infrared thermometer market comprises two primary types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead type thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure surface temperature quickly and non-invasively, making them suitable for screening large groups. In contrast, ear type thermometers measure the infrared heat emitted from the eardrum, providing accurate core body temperature readings. Both types are widely used in clinical settings and at home for fever detection, offering convenience and fast results for healthcare monitoring.</p></p>
