<p><ul><li>One Initiating Circuit</li><li>Multi-initiating Circuits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Conventional Fire Alarm System market is categorized into two primary types: One Initiating Circuit and Multi-Initiating Circuits. One Initiating Circuit systems utilize a single detection point, making them simple and cost-effective, ideal for smaller spaces. In contrast, Multi-Initiating Circuits incorporate multiple detection points, allowing for more comprehensive coverage and quicker response times across larger or more complex environments. This scalability and flexibility enhances safety and compliance with fire codes, catering to diverse customer needs in various settings.</p></p>
