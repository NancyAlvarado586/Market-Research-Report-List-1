<p><p>The textured soy protein concentrates market includes various forms, primarily granules and flour. Granules are small, coarse particles that offer a chewy texture, making them ideal for meat alternatives and as meat extenders in dishes. Flour, on the other hand, consists of finely ground soy protein and is often used as a binding agent in recipes, enhancing nutritional content while providing a smooth texture. Both types are popular due to their versatility in food applications and health benefits as plant-based protein sources.</p></p>
