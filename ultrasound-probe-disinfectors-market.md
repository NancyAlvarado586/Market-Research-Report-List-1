<p><p>The Ultrasound Probe Disinfectors market encompasses various disinfection types, categorized primarily into Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs) and High-Level Disinfection (HLD). LLDs/ILDs are designed to eliminate most pathogens, making them suitable for probes used on intact skin, while HLD methods ensure the destruction of all microorganisms, including spores, for more invasive procedures. Both categories are critical for maintaining hygiene in medical settings, reducing infection risk and enhancing patient safety through effective equipment sanitation.</p></p>
