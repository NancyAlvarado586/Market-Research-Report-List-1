<p><p>Self-aligning ball bearings are specialized rolling bearings that can accommodate misalignment between the shaft and housing. They feature two rows of balls and a spherical outer ring raceway, allowing for angular misalignment without compromising performance. This adaptability enhances their application in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, where equipment may experience misalignment over time.</p><p>The Self-aligning Ball Bearing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
