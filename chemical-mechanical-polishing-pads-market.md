<p><p>In the Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads market, there are two primary types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are designed for higher material removal rates and are typically used for polishing hard substrates, such as silicon wafers. They provide excellent flatness but may cause more scratches. In contrast, soft CMP pads are more forgiving, offering better conformability to irregular surfaces and reduced defects, making them ideal for delicate applications and achieving high precision in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
