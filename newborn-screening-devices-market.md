<p><p>The newborn screening devices market encompasses various testing methodologies to identify potential health issues in infants. The Dry Blood Spot Test involves collecting a small blood sample to screen for metabolic and genetic disorders. The Hearing Screen Test assesses auditory function to detect hearing loss early, crucial for language development. The Critical Congenital Heart Disease (CCHD) Test uses pulse oximetry to identify heart defects by measuring oxygen levels in the blood, facilitating timely interventions for affected newborns.</p></p>
