<p><p>The Automotive Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) system market primarily consists of three types: Radar, LIDAR, and Sensor Fusion technologies. Radar systems utilize radio waves to detect vehicle distance and speed, ensuring safe navigation. LIDAR employs laser light to create detailed 3D maps of the surrounding environment, offering high precision in object detection. Sensor Fusion combines data from multiple sources, including cameras and ultrasonic sensors, to enhance situational awareness and improve decision-making, leading to more reliable and efficient ACC systems.</p></p>
