<p><strong>The In-Wheel Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Type</li><li>Outer Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Wheel Motors are categorized into two main types: Inner Rotor Type and Outer Rotor Type. The Inner Rotor Type features the rotor inside the stator, providing a compact design and efficient torque delivery. This design is generally favored for enhanced performance and cooling. On the other hand, the Outer Rotor Type places the rotor outside the stator, resulting in a larger diameter and potentially higher torque output. Each type offers distinct advantages for electric vehicle applications, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
