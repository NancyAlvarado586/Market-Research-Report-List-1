<p><p>Mobile wallets can be categorized into two primary market types: remote payment and NFC (Near Field Communication) payment. Remote payment wallets enable users to make online transactions from anywhere using their smartphones, facilitating e-commerce and digital purchases. In contrast, NFC wallets function through close-range communication, allowing users to make in-store payments by tapping their devices on terminals. Both types enhance convenience, security, and efficiency in payment processing, catering to varying consumer needs in the digital payment landscape.</p></p>
