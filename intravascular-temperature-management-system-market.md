<p><strong>The Intravascular Temperature Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intravascular Warming</li><li>Intravascular Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intravascular Temperature Management System market is divided into two main types: intravascular warming and intravascular cooling. Intravascular warming systems are designed to elevate a patient's core body temperature, often used in surgeries or trauma to prevent hypothermia. Conversely, intravascular cooling systems lower a patient's temperature, typically utilized in critical care settings to manage conditions like cardiac arrest or brain injury. Both technologies aim to maintain optimal body temperature, enhancing patient outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
