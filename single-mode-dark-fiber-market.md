<p><p>The Single Mode Dark Fiber market is primarily categorized into two types based on the material used: glass and plastic. Glass fiber offers superior performance for long-distance communication due to its low attenuation and higher bandwidth capabilities, making it ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, plastic fiber, while typically less expensive and easier to install, is suited for shorter distances and lower bandwidth applications, appealing to consumer markets and less demanding environments. Both options cater to different needs in connectivity.</p></p>
