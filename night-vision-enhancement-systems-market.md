<p><p>The Night Vision Enhancement Systems market is primarily categorized into Near-infrared (NIR) and Far-infrared (FIR) technologies. NIR systems utilize wavelengths just beyond the visible spectrum, enhancing visibility in low light by amplifying ambient light, making them ideal for tactical applications. In contrast, FIR systems detect thermal radiation and create images based on temperature differences, effectively functioning in complete darkness and adverse weather conditions. Both types serve distinct purposes across military, surveillance, and consumer sectors, enhancing situational awareness and safety.</p></p>
