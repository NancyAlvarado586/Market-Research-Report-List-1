<p><ul><li>Modified Sine Wave Type</li><li>Pure Sine Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive power inverters market features two main types: modified sine wave and pure sine wave inverters. Modified sine wave inverters produce a waveform that approximates a sine wave but has more harmonic distortion, making them suitable for basic electronics and tools. In contrast, pure sine wave inverters generate a smooth, consistent waveform similar to natural electricity, making them ideal for sensitive electronics and appliances that require clean power. This distinction drives consumer preferences based on application needs and device compatibility.</p></p>
