<p><ul><li>CT</li><li>PET&SPECT</li><li>Composite System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dual Power Switches market includes three primary types: CT (Current Transformer), PET (Positive Temperature Coefficient), and SPECT (Spatially Enhanced Composite Technology) switches, along with composite systems. CT switches are typically used for current monitoring, whereas PET switches enhance thermal management in electrical devices. SPECT combines spatially varied designs for improved functionality. Composite systems integrate multiple technologies for advanced power control and reliability, catering to various industrial applications and enhancing overall energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
