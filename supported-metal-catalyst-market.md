<p><p>Supported metal catalysts are essential in various chemical processes, classified into two main types: single metal catalysts and multimetallic catalysts. Single metal catalysts consist of one type of metal, typically offering high activity and selectivity for specific reactions. In contrast, multimetallic catalysts contain two or more metals, allowing for enhanced catalytic properties, improved reaction pathways, and greater efficiency. These catalysts find widespread applications in petroleum refining, chemical synthesis, and environmental processes, driving advancements in industrial efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
