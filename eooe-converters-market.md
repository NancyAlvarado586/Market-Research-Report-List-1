<p><p>The EOOE (Electrical Optical Optical Electrical) converters market encompasses two main types: single mode and multimode. Single mode converters are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path for minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, multimode converters support shorter distances and multiple light paths, enhancing bandwidth capacity within local networks. Each type serves specific application needs, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness in optical fiber communication systems.</p></p>
