<p><p>The medical liquid chromatography market primarily consists of two key types: Liquid Chromatography (LC) and Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS). LC is widely used for separating and analyzing compounds in various biological samples, playing a crucial role in drug development and clinical diagnostics. LC-MS combines liquid chromatography with mass spectrometry, offering enhanced sensitivity and specificity, making it invaluable for detecting low-abundance substances and complex mixtures. Both types are essential for accurate analysis in medical research and healthcare applications.</p></p>
